Theodora Skipitares

Six Characters

With a nod to Pirandello, Six Characters, 2016 (A Family Album) explores ideas of family, reality versus illusion, and displacement. Live music by Sxip Shirey/Jordan Morton, video and giant puppet figures, including Margaret Mead, a variety of Pinocchios and Pirandello himself, highlight the evening.
