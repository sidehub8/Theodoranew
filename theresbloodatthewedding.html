There's Blood at the Wedding:

Songs for Lorca.


2018 There's Blood at the Wedding: Songs for Lorca. Lorca's plays, especially BLOOD WEDDING, focus on endless cycles of violence within a community. In this production, his texts are juxtaposed with contemporary accounts in which men and women have been killed by police. Music by Sxip Shirey.
